About Me

Where Strategy
Meets Architecture

I'm a senior Data & AI executive with over 25 years of experience leading enterprise-scale transformations across financial services, telecommunications, and cloud technology.

Most recently as Field CDO / Head of Data & Analytics at Google Cloud, I led AI and data transformations for major US financial institutions — driving $850M+ in GCP commitments and scaling AI revenue from $15M to $500M.

My work sits at the intersection of technical architecture and business value. I build platforms, teams, and operating models that turn data from a cost center into a competitive weapon.

I hold an MBA in Finance from UMass Amherst and an M.S. from Clemson University. US citizen, federal clearance eligible, based in Reston, Virginia.
